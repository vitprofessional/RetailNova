### Method 1: Sidebar Menu (Recommended)
1. Open your RetailNova dashboard
2. Look for the **"Reports"** menu item in the left sidebar
3. Click to expand and see all 6 report options
4. Click any report to view it

Navigate to these URLs in your browser:
- Reports Dashboard: `http://your-site.com/reports`

## üé® Understanding the Display

### Color Coding
- **Blue Cards** - Information/totals
- **Green Cards** - Positive metrics (high sales, in stock)
- **Yellow Cards** - Warnings (low stock, partial payment)
- **Red Cards** - Problems (out of stock, unpaid)

### Status Badges
- `Paid` (Green) - Fully paid
- `Partial` (Yellow) - Partially paid
- `Due/Unpaid` (Red) - Not paid
- `In Stock` (Green) - Adequate quantity
- `Low Stock` (Yellow) - Below alert level
- `Out of Stock` (Red) - Zero or negative

### Pagination
- Reports showing many items are split into pages (50 per page)
- Use "Next" and "Previous" buttons to navigate
- Page numbers shown at bottom

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why is data not showing in my report?**
A: Make sure you have sales/purchase data in your system. Try changing the date range to include older data.

**Q: Can I export reports to Excel?**
A: Print the report as a PDF first. Most PDF viewers can copy/export table data.

**Q: How often should I run these reports?**
A: Suggestions:
- Business Report: Monthly
- Sale Report: Weekly
- Top Customers: Monthly
- Payable/Receivable: Weekly
- Stock Report: Twice weekly

**Q: Can other users access reports?**
A: Only admin users can access reports. This is for security.

**Q: Why is profit showing as $0?**
A: Check that your purchases are linked to sales properly. Profit is calculated from invoice items.

**Q: How do I change the report period?**
A: Use the date filter at the top of each report.
