# RetailNova
## Smart POS + Accounting + Expense Control for Growing Retail Businesses

RetailNova helps modern retailers and service businesses run daily operations from one platform—sales, inventory, customer management, accounting, expenses, warranty tracking, reporting, and role-based control.

---

## Why RetailNova?

- **All-in-one operations:** Replace disconnected tools with one unified system.
- **Faster checkout and service:** Manage sales, returns, quotations, and service jobs efficiently.
- **Real-time financial visibility:** Track cashflow, expenses, ledgers, and key reports instantly.
- **Stronger control and accountability:** Built-in auditing, permissions, and role-based access.

---

## Core Modules

### Sales & Customer Operations
- New Sale and Sale List
- Sale Returns
- Customer and Supplier Management
- Quotation Creation and Tracking

### Product & Inventory Management
- Product Catalog (Brand, Category, Unit)
- Stock Tracking
- Purchase and Purchase Return Management
- Damage Product Tracking
- Serial and Warranty (RMA) Management

### Accounting & Expense Management
- Chart of Accounts
- Double-Entry Transactions
- Financial Reports (Balance Sheet, Income Statement, Trial Balance)
- Expense Categories, Expense Entry, and Receipt Uploads
- Payment Method Tracking (Cash, Bank, Card, Cheque, Mobile)

### Business Administration
- Multi-role User Management
- Business Settings and Location Management
- Audit Logs and Activity Tracking
- Dashboard and Performance Reports

---

## Business Benefits

- **Improve profitability:** Identify high-margin products and control expense leakage.
- **Reduce operational errors:** Standardized workflows across purchases, sales, and accounts.
- **Accelerate decisions:** Access reports for sales, stock, payables/receivables, and top customers.
- **Scale with confidence:** Role-based controls support growing teams and multi-location needs.

---

## Who Is RetailNova For?

- Retail stores and chain outlets
- Electronics and device sellers with serial/warranty requirements
- Service + product businesses that need combined workflows
- SMEs looking to move from manual records to integrated digital operations

---

## Technology & Reliability

- Built on Laravel 12 + PHP 8.2
- Secure authentication and CSRF protection
- Change history with auditing support
- Storage support for receipts and attachments
- Data-table powered screens for fast filtering and reporting
